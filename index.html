Hey there! My name is Mark, better known as Bormethius

I'm a livestreamer based in Cleveland, OH.


I livestream on the platform Twitch, where I livestream multiple games and do unique streams at times, for example, a cooking stream or lego stream. I organize many community events and have a Discord server to assist with that. My livestreams are constantly moderated by a team of moderators who don't tolerate anything that might be seen as discriminatory.
